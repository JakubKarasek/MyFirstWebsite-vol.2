Neil Young

Neil Percival Young is a world-famous Canadian singer-songwriter. He is an influential guitarist in addition to being a screenwriter, producer and director. His remarkable, transcendent style has set him as a one of the greatest artists of all time.

  • Born on November 12, 1945 in Toronto
  • In early 1969, he collaborated with a band ’Crazy Horse’ and released his famous album, ‘Everybody Knows This Is Nowhere’.
  • He has released numerous notable albums which achieved both critical and commercial success.
